Instant Pot Whole Milk Yogurt

Creamy homemade yogurt with optional honey, vanilla, and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 gallon whole milk
  • 2–3 tablespoons dry milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons plain nonfat Chobani yogurt

Optional Flavorings

  • 1–3 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2–1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Method

  1. Whisk milk and dry milk in the Instant Pot liner.
  2. Use Yogurt → Boil to heat the milk.
  3. Cool to 110–115°F.
  4. Mix starter with a little warm milk, then stir back in.
  5. Incubate on Yogurt setting for 8–10 hours.
  6. Chill at least 4 hours before serving.

Athlete Sourdough Loaf

A soft, higher-protein sandwich loaf for breakfast and training days.

Ingredients

  • 500 g bread flour
  • 50 g whole wheat flour
  • 100 g active sourdough starter
  • 350 g warm milk
  • 30 g honey
  • 40 g butter or olive oil
  • 20 g dry milk powder
  • 10 g salt

Method

  1. Mix milk, starter, honey, and flour into a shaggy dough.
  2. Rest 20 minutes.
  3. Add salt and butter, then knead until smooth.
  4. Bulk ferment 4–6 hours.
  5. Shape into a loaf pan and proof 1–2 hours.
  6. Bake at 375°F for 35–40 minutes.

Peanut Butter Swirl Sourdough S’mores Cookies

Soft bakery-style cookies with discard, chocolate, graham, and marshmallow.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 150 g brown sugar
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 100 g sourdough discard
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 180 g flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup mini marshmallows

Method

  1. Cream butter and sugars.
  2. Mix in egg, vanilla, and discard.
  3. Add dry ingredients.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips, graham crackers, and marshmallows.
  5. Swirl a little peanut butter on top.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 10–12 minutes.

Apple Fritter Sourdough Discard

A warm fried treat with diced apples and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sourdough discard
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch salt
  • 1 apple, diced

Method

  1. Whisk together wet ingredients.
  2. Stir in dry ingredients.
  3. Fold in apples.
  4. Drop spoonfuls into hot oil.
  5. Fry until golden on both sides.

Protein Energy Balls

Chocolatey grab-and-go bites for a simple snack or bakery breakfast case.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 2 tablespoons chopped walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips

Method

  1. Mix everything in a bowl.
  2. Add a little extra honey if needed.
  3. Roll into bite-sized balls.
  4. Chill before serving.

Note: sunflower seeds can turn bitter if over-toasted, so walnuts, coconut, or extra chocolate are easier toppings.

English Muffin Breadcrumb Chicken Drumsticks

Crispy oven-baked drumsticks using homemade English muffin crumbs.

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup English muffin breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Coat drumsticks in flour, egg wash, then breadcrumbs.
  3. Place on a lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake 35–40 minutes until golden and cooked through.
